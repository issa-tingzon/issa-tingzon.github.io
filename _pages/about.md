<br>
I'm Issa, a Doctoral Researcher at <a href='https://www.ri.se/en'>RISE Research Institutes of Sweden</a> and a PhD Student at <a href='http://kth.se/'>KTH Royal Institute of Technology</a> under <a href='https://www.kth.se/profile/yifang'>Prof. Yifang Ban</a> and <a href='https://aleksispi.github.io/'>Dr. Aleksis Pirinen</a>. Previously, I was a a Data Science Consultant at <a href='https://www.unicef.org/'>UNICEF</a> and a Disaster & Climate Risk Data Fellow at <a href='https://www.worldbank.org/en/home'>The World Bank</a>/<a href='https://www.gfdrr.org/en'>GFDRR</a>. I'm also a core member of <a href='https://www.climatechange.ai/'>Climate Change AI</a>, and a volunteer at <a href="https://www.womenwhocode.com/">Women Who Code</a>. 

My work focuses mainly on the applications of machine learning, computer vision, and Earth observation for socioeconomic development, humanitarian assistance, climate resilience, and disaster response.
